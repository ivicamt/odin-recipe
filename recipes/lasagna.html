The Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound (450g) ground beef or turkey
  2. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 24 ounces (680g) marinara sauce
  5. 1 cup ricotta cheese
  6. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  8. 1 tablespoon dried basil
  9. 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  10. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  11. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  12. 6-8 no-boil lasagna noodles
  13. Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  • Stir in the marinara sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  • In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, half of the shredded mozzarella, and half of the grated Parmesan.
  • To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 2-3 lasagna noodles on top, covering the sauce.
  • Spoon half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles and spread it evenly. Top with another layer of meat sauce.
  • Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  • Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Serve and enjoy your quick and delicious refor lasagna!
