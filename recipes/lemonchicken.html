Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. Rinse the whole chicken inside and out with cold water and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Cut one of the lemons into slices and set them aside.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, olive oil, dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  5. Rub the chicken all over, including inside the cavity, with the prepared marinade, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  6. Place the lemon slices inside the chicken cavity.
  7. Truss the chicken (optional) - tie the legs together with kitchen twine to help it cook more evenly.
  8. Place the chicken on a roasting pan or a baking dish with a rack, breast-side up.
  9. Squeeze the juice of the second lemon all over the chicken.
  10. Pour the chicken broth or water into the bottom of the roasting pan to keep the chicken moist during cooking.
  11. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) and the skin is golden and crispy.
  12. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.
  13. Serve the lemon garlic roasted chicken with your favorite sides like roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad.

Enjoy your delicious Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken! The citrusy lemon flavor and aromatic herbs make this dish both flavorful and comforting.
